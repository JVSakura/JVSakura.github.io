About Me

My name is James Alexander Vasquez. I am a full-stack web development student and this is my portfolio.

I am very new to the world of web development and coding. Traditionally I have been a very analog type of student with a pre-medical background. I have previous work experience as an Emergency Medical Technician (EMT) as well as a Medical Scribe that worked in a Physician's clinic. During these difficult times that we are facing I found it pertinent to add new skills to my repertoire and thus here I am. Necessity is the mother of invention after all.

I find it important for myself to exercise daily, and in my spare time I like to read or play video games. I have traveled outside the country several times and have a bit of wanderlust. The photo in my profile is a live photo I took myself while visiting Japan.

Working in the medical field I find it to be a necessity to make the public more aware of health information and the resources they can use to their advantage. I hope to utilize my web development skills to help bridge this gap.
